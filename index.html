About Miss Dior

Miss Dior Eau de Parfum is a reimagined fragrance built around the beautiful Centifolia Rose. It is designed for women who value sophistication and timeless beauty.

Fragrance Notes

  • Fresh Lily-of-the-Valley
  • Powdery Iris
  • Apricot-Tinged Peony Accord
  • Musk, Sweet Vanilla, and Sandalwood

Signature Elements
